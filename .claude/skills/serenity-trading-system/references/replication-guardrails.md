## Reflexivity discount (mandatory for any name he has touched)

His posts move small caps 10-20% the same day; he jokes "people think I'm the
catalyst". Post-2026Q1 wins are partially self-fulfilling — a copier buys his
flywheel, not his alpha. Rules:

1. Record **days-since-his-first-post** on the name; same-week entries after a
   viral post default to 避免追高.
2. Classify the post type before acting: new bottleneck thesis (highest
   weight) > reaffirmation/buy-the-dip > supplier map / no-position idea
   (research input only) > victory lap (the mispricing is gone).
3. Check whether institutions already arrived (13F/13G, Nordic short
   registers, block trades) — institutions-in means the re-rating largely
   happened.
4. Typical thesis-to-validation lag is 5-60 trading days — patience beats
   speed; same-day copying is the worst of both worlds.
5. A fresh name that passes the checklist deserves MORE weight than an echo
   of his existing pick.

- Independent re-scores of dated public calls: ~61% 30-day directional
  accuracy; ~41% strict ±10% hits; mature photonics/InP/memory bottleneck
  theses ~75-85% at least partly validated. Use as rough priors.

  supplier-map/no-position posts > victory laps.

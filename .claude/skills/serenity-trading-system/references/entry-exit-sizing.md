Distilled 2026-07-03 from the full corpus (96 entry-discipline tweets, 79

## Entry

His rules (all transfer):

1. **Forward clock**: markets price 8-12 months ahead. A 2028 ramp starts
   pricing in H2 2026 — buy at qualification stage (design wins, foundry
   partnerships, guidance language) while reported revenue is still ~0, with
   the catalyst ≥12 months out. "You won't get 10x if it's already priced in."
2. **Against-consensus timing**: best entries coincide with "meme stock" /
   "scam" ridicule, sell-side bubble calls, or media-framing crashes on
   committed multi-year capex (algorithmic risk-off ≠ fundamental re-rating).
3. **Catalyst pre-positioning**: place longs BEFORE dated catalysts (GTC, OFC,
   Computex, earnings, MSCI inclusion, policy votes), enter on drops.
4. **Anti-FOMO**: never chase the bottleneck-of-the-week news cycle. To join a
   hot theme, buy the same chain's not-yet-moved names, or wait for the flush
   ("Fear > fundamentals" days are entry windows).
5. **Entry bands**: each idea carries an ideal entry price ("NBIS <$100",
   "HIMS <$45"), re-rated weekly. Short-term entry matters ±10-20%; if the
   thesis is a 10x, a missed perfect entry is not a reason to skip.

System rule: same, plus record days-since-any-Serenity-post on the name and
apply the reflexivity discount (replication-guardrails.md) before entry.

## Sizing

His observed structure (2026-02-07 disclosure, sums to ~115% = his margin):

- **Theme sleeves**: 10-35% per theme (Memory 35%, Digital assets 25%,
  Datacenter 15%, Photonics 10%…).
- **Name blocks inside a sleeve**: 2.5% / 5% / 10% steps.
- **Tracker tail**: 0.2-1% starter positions on watchlist names.
- **Conviction tiers** S/A/B/C/D/F per sector; "fundamentally de-risked"
  (Mag7 counterparty + signed take-or-pay) = top tier and bigger size.
- **Options**: long-dated leaps only at low IV (~10-35%); calls INSTEAD of
  shares when the equity has genuine zero-risk (e.g. China export ban) —
  defined-risk substitution, not leverage for its own sake.
- **His overrides (DO NOT COPY)**: single names pushed to 30% (NBIS), a
  claimed 50% (BTC); ~1.15-1.4x portfolio margin; de-levering only on
  discretionary macro judgment (went to zero margin on Iran escalation).

System rule: keep the sleeve/block/tracker structure; cap single names and
sleeves per the user's stated risk budget (ask if unknown; never default to
his overrides); no margin in recommendations; diversify by RETURN DRIVER, not
ticker count — his own June 2026 lesson ("Diversified Losses": many tickers,
one CPO factor, -35~40%).

## Exit

Fact first: his core positions are effectively never sold ("70%+ of my
portfolio is unrealized", "haven't sold a single share" of SIVE), there are NO
price stops, and held losers include VIRT -45% and OPAD -36%. The six
situations where he actually sells or de-risks:

| Trigger | His action |
|---|---|
| Price reaches his forward-model band ceiling | Downgrade to hold/sell; sell covered calls at that strike rather than dump shares |
| Forward numbers deteriorate (guidance/margin cut, ramp delay — e.g. ASTS launch slip hurting forward growth) | The only "warranted" sale. Price falling with thesis intact = not a reason |
| Better opportunity (relative) | Swap loser thesis into winner (sold CIFR/WULF → consolidated NBIS; "would sell TSLA here — better opportunities elsewhere") |
| Structural red flag appears | Exit + public bear turn (large ATM filing, SBC-alongside-raise, serial diluter management) |
| Special position types | Swing trades exit next-day; catalyst trades exit on the event; squeeze plays have NO price target — exit on the volume-spike blowoff |
| Macro regime break | De-lever (1.4x → 0) and hedge, but keep core shares |

System rule (mandatory replacements when advising):

- Every recommendation carries BOTH a **price stop** (ATR/support-based,
  loss-capped) and a **thesis stop** (the observable event that invalidates
  the bottleneck). His no-stop style requires his daily information flow;
  without it, "hold through -40%" is riding unverified losers.
- Risk/reward at entry ≥ 2:1 against the base target, else downgrade to
  watch.
- Take-profit path mirrors his: trim/covered-call at the band ceiling, full
  exit on thesis stop or red flag — but the price stop is never optional.
- Regime kill-switch (replication-guardrails.md) overrides everything: freeze
  theme adds when 2+ AI-capex leading indicators deteriorate.
